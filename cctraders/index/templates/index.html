Who We Are

Welcome to CCTraders, where innovation meets sustainability, and every endeavor is a commitment to a better future. As a multifaceted company, we specialize in waste management services, agricultural solutions, and efficient logistics that seamlessly connect the threads of our operations.

At CCTraders, we envision a Lesotho where responsible business practices drive positive change. Our mission goes beyond commerce; it's about nurturing the environment, uplifting communities, and fostering a resilient agricultural sector.
